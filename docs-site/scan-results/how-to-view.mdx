Once a scan is running, open it to watch progress and browse findings as they land. The
**Overview** dashboard and the **Scans** list are your entry points.

## The scan detail page

Open any scan to see:

- **Header** - the target repo (with a remote/local badge), the workflow, model, harness,
  thinking effort, and commit.
- **Stats** - number of findings, how many are confirmed exploitable, the primary
  post-script, and how many agent skills were used.
- **Status** - a live badge (`queued`, `pending`, `prewarming_cache`, `running`,
  `rate_limited`, `post_processing`, `completed`, `paused`, `stopped`, or `failed`) plus
  available controls to pause, resume, or export. Queued scans wait for active scans;
  rate-limited scans show when their automatic retry is due.
- **Findings** - the list of vulnerabilities, ordered by your ranker and annotated with
  [chips](/post-scripts/chips) from post-scripts.

## The findings list

Each row summarizes a finding - its title, location, and a few chips. Findings are sorted by
the ranking rules you set on the scan, so the highest-priority results sit at the top.

Click any finding to open the [vulnerability view](/scan-results/vulnerability-view).

<Note>
  Findings appear as the scan progresses - you don't have to wait for `completed` to start
  triaging what's already come in.
</Note>
