---
title: "Rankers on a scan"
description: "Compose the ranking rules that order this scan's findings."
---

Every scan needs ranking rules. You compose them in the scan form from saved
[severity rankers](/severity-ranker/why) plus optional scan-specific rules.

## Composing the rules

- Select **any number** of saved rankers - their rules are concatenated in the order you
  pick them.
- Add optional **scan-specific rules** in the free-text box; they're appended after the
  selected rankers.

The combined text is what the model uses to order this scan's findings. The result can't be
empty - provide at least one saved ranker or some scan-specific rules.

## When ranking runs

After the workflow finishes and findings are de-duplicated, the ranker orders the canonical
findings by priority. That ordering is what you see in the scan's findings list, and it
drives which findings surface first.

<Note>
  Keep a stable "house" ranker for your program and layer target-specific rules on top per
  scan - no need to edit the saved ranker each time.
</Note>
